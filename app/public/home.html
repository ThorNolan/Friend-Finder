Desperate for some new friends? Real friends? Fake friends? Does it even matter?

It's a lonely world we live in. We all know that. More and more, people are turning to applications as a means to connect with one another. You can think of this as something like that. Or you can think about it as something completely different. It hardly matters, because, when it comes down to it, these people may or may not even be real. But the excitement and satisfaction you feel when you make a connection—ephemeral and meaningless as it may be—can do its small part in staving off the crushing and inescapable loneliness of existence.

All you have to do is provide your name and a photo, and respond to ten statements, and you'll be that much closer to a fleeting yet beautiful respite from your isolation. Let yourself forget for a moment about the inevitability of your own death, take the survey, and feel that rush of genuine* human connection. Say goodbye to loneliness, even if it's just for a moment.
