Desperate for some new friends? Real friends? Fake friends? Does it even matter?

It's a lonely world we live in. We all know that. More and more, people are turning to applications as a means to connect with one another. You can think of this as something like that. Or you can think about it as something different. It hardly matters, because, when it comes down to it, these people may or may not even be real. But connections can be ephemeral--the rush you feel when that connection is made is almost enough. So take our survey and feel that rush of a genuine* human connection. Say goobye to loneliness, even if it's just for a moment.
